# **App Name**: SafePath Fitness

## Core Features:

- Workout Sheet Display: Visually highlight the workout sheet with an emphasis on clarity for beginners.
- Exercise Video Integration: Provide embedded exercise execution videos as a 'safe guide'.
- Personalized Workout Plan: Generative AI tool provides customized workout plans based on user's fitness level and goals. Plans are suitable for users that will train on their own.
- Progress Tracking: Enable users to track their progress and visualize improvements over time.
- Exercise logging: Enable users to log workout sets and reps, and workout date

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft, desaturated blue (#A0D2EB) to inspire trust and calmness. This color choice supports the theme of a clear and trustworthy fitness app, steering away from the intense, energetic tones often seen in generic fitness apps, and aligns with a 'you are on the right path' feeling.
- Background color: Warm, light neutral (#F5F5DC) for a welcoming, uncluttered backdrop.
- Accent color: Muted green (#C2E1C2) for subtle highlights and progress indicators.
- Font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) for a simple and human feel, ensuring excellent readability.
- Use friendly icons to visually guide users through each step of their workout.
- Clean interface with minimal elements per screen to maintain focus on the main action.
- Subtle animations and transitions to reinforce a sense of progression and achievement.
